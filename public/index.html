Hi I'm Sean - I starting making music for fun over the past few years, and at some point made it a goal to release some of it. I just put out the first song as Unicorn Taxidermy, and if you're here I guess you heard it... thanks for checking it out!

Btw, if you see videos/songs here or there by XANHAN, that was the original name of this project. Until I realized it wasn't a very good one... Unicorn Taxidermy just feels like more of a vibe, if only because it's easier to remember! And through COVID/lockdown, I've grown a collection of ideas that I'm pumped to get out there over the course of 2021 and beyond.
